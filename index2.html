City Description:

Madrid is the capital and largest city of Spain, located in the center of the country. It is one of the most important cultural and tourist centers in Europe. The city is famous for its lively atmosphere, beautiful streets, and buildings that combine tradition and modernity. Life in Madrid is always full of energy, both day and night, and it is known for its friendly people who love music and celebrations.
